## Company Financial Highlights

### Layton Construction
- **2024 Revenue:** $7.1B total, $2.4B in Southwest region
- **Ranking:** #1 on ENR Southwest Top Contractors, #1 on Utah 100 (5th consecutive year)
- **Notable:** Named MWCN 2024 Entrepreneur of the Year (David S. Layton)

### Big-D Construction
- **2024 Revenue:** $1.923B (ENR Top 400 #35)
- **2025 Revenue:** Approaching $3B
- **Growth:** Big-D Signature grew from $36M to $132M over 5 years
- **Lindon Office:** On track for ~$300M in 2025

### Okland Construction
- **2024 Revenue:** $1.76B total, $1.026B regional
- **Ranking:** #4 on ENR Intermountain Top Contractors
- **Notable:** ENR Intermountain Contractor of the Year 2024

### Jacobsen Construction
- **2024 Revenue:** $1B+
- **2022 Revenue:** $862M (record year)
- **Ranking:** ENR Top 400 #131 (22nd consecutive year)

### Staker Parson Companies
- **2023 Revenue:** $574M (16% YoY growth)
- **Notable:** Major asphalt and aggregate supplier in Utah

### Geneva Rock Products
- **2024 Revenue:** $721.5M
- **Parent:** Clyde Companies

### Clyde Companies (Parent)
- **2022 Revenue:** ~$2.5B
- **Subsidiaries:** WW Clyde, Geneva Rock, Sunroc/Suncore
- **Notable:** One of Utah's largest family-owned businesses
